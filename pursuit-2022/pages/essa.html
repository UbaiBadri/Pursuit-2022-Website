Troll By Tronics

This Event will be held in three rounds:

Round 1- This round will be based on basic electrical/electronics quiz questions (aptitude test).The aptitude test will be on Google forms .The team with less number of marks will be disqualified.
Round 2- In the round, you have to solve problem statement given by us. you have to draw circuit as per problem statement .This round is to test your practical knowledge.
Round 3- In this round, there will be the fastest finger round take place and from that, the participants will be selected for the hot seat of KBCK(Kon Banega Circuit King).In KBCK there will be MCQ type questioning related to electronics and the rules are similar to KBC .
