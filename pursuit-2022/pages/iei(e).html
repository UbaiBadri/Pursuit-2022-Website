Technophilia

The Technophilia is a kind of event which will send you on a journey of full of enjoyment and lot of learning stuff. It will test the core knowledge along with coding and design skills of participants considering almost all engineering domains. The event is divided into three segments. Participants must come in pair(team of two member). This Event will be held in three rounds:

Round 1- MIND TENTACLE
In Mind Tentacle participants must have to appear for three subrounds,particularly based on basic technical engineering knowledge of all domains.
The sub-rounds are as follows:

  1. Quiz
  2. Component Identification
  3. Crosswords

Round 2- BLIND DEBUGGER
In Blind Debugger both team member must have to debugg a code. The twist is one of the member is blindfolded and he is completely depend on other memberâ€™s instruction.
Round 3- REALTIME TERROR
This round will test the design skills of the participants. In this round Teams will have to design a poster on a given theme and according to judges rating top 2 teams will win the event.
