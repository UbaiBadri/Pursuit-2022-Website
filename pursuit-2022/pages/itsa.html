CodeStorm

This is a technical event which is based on the strategical skill as well as coding skills of the participants. With presence of mind and knowing the ropes one can tackle this event. If one can crack the first two rounds the door for final round will be opened for them. The event will be held online or offline.
This Event will be held in three rounds:

Round 1-TECHNO-QUIZ

  • Platform: Google form.

Round 2-LOGICAL-NUT
