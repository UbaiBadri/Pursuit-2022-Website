SAE(Team X-Treme)




CAD Designing Contest

In event, the participants would be given 2D drawing for a 3D part where they need to use CAD software. The group which complete the parts within the stipulated time will be declared as winner.

Details Of Event

Rules

Rules for the Event are:

  • Participants can use any CAD Software.
  • Two 2D drawings will be provided for 3D parts to participants.
  • Total time allotted for the event is 1 hour.
  • The one who use less commands and complete the task fully defined with less required time will be winner.
  • Any discrepancy leads to disqualification of the participant.
  • Participants should have their own systems with req. software installed, our system will be provided but they have to manage with available software on it.
  • For events with less than 12 entries, only First prize will be awarded.
  • If the entries are less than 5 then that particular event will be cancelled.
  • Participants should follow COVID guidelines.
