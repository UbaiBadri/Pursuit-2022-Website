CID (Code Investigation Department)

Code Investigation Departmant is a kind of online treasure hunt game in which you are so called CID officer and you have to solve the case by analising hint and clue given to you. This Event will be held in two rounds:

Round 1 - Being ACP Pradyuman - In this round you will be given Some basics maths ,Aptitude General Knowledge puzzles, also basic coding puzzles you have to solve puzzles using hints given and go front in game those who will solve the all puzzles fastest (according to time) and will be Out off maze first will be winner of this round.
Round 2 - Kuch toh Gadbad he daya - In this round you will be provided data .it can be image or text or code you have to find that "gadbad" in that and correct it by doing this you will go further in game And again one who will solve the maze and out of game (according to time) will be winner.

Rules

Rules for the Event are:

  • There will be 2 round and the students who crack round 1 will get selected for round 2.
  • The selection is based on time taken by participant to solve the maze so you need to be faster.
  • There will be single participation.
  • For events with less than 12 entries, only First prize will be awarded.
  • If the entries are less than 5 then that particular event will be cancelled.
  • Participants should follow COVID guidelines.
