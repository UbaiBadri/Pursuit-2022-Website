CREATIVE JUGAD

CREATIVE JUGAD is an event for creative minds to develop their researches and innovative ideas to be technology changer and just it flow by the means of presentation and words where the students can share their knowledge and improvise their entrepreneurship skills. CREATIVE JUGAD provides a platform for budding entrepreneurs to unleash their ideas in front of esteemed panel of Judges.

Details Of Event

Rules

Rules for the Event are:

  • Your work should be original and in the specified format.
  • Interested candidates are advised to mail the presentation in .ppt or .pptx format.
  • The competition is open to all Engineering and Non-Engineering Students.
  • Decision of the Judges and the event heads shall be treated as final and binding on all and cannot be contested.
  • Directly copying from the Internet is strongly discouraged and will not be entertained
  • All the candidates will get participation certificates.
  • The participants should come with their ID Card. /li>
  • Spot Entries are also accepted.
  • For any clarification or further details the interested candidates can contact the Faculty or Student Coordinators.
  • Jury decision will be final decision.
  • All the rights are reserved with IEI MECH event committee.
  • Please mention your sector in which you will be presenting your ideas on above the presentation submitted in the given mail.>

Presentation Rules:

  • The Idea submitted will have to be pr esented during the event.
  • Hard copies of the same are to be submitted before presentation to Judges.
  • The Teams will get 10 minutes to present their Idea and 5 minutes will be for questioning by judges.
  • The participants will have to present their papers in MS-Power Point (ppt) format only.
  • Violation of any rule can result in rejection of Idea.
  • The organizers reserve the right to change/update the rules of the contest at any point of time and will do their best to inform to participants of the same. However, it is ultimately the responsibility of the teams to keep themselves updated.
